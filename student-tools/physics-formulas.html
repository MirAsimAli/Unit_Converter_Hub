Physics Formulas for Students

This physics formulas page provides essential equations used in mechanics, motion, force, energy and electricity. These formulas are useful for school students, competitive exams, engineering entrance preparation, and quick revision.

These physics equations are structured for quick learning and fast reference, making this page a powerful student study tool.

Motion & Kinematics

Speed = Distance / Time
Velocity = Displacement / Time
Acceleration = (v − u) / Time
v = u + at
s = ut + ½at²

Force & Newton's Laws

Force = Mass × Acceleration (F = ma)
Weight = Mass × Gravity (W = mg)
Momentum = Mass × Velocity

Work, Energy & Power

Work = Force × Distance
Kinetic Energy = ½mv²
Potential Energy = mgh
Power = Work / Time

Electricity

Voltage = Current × Resistance (V = IR)
Power = Voltage × Current
